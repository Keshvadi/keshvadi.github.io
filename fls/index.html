Free Live Sports Streaming: Exploring User Experiences and Security Concerns

Introduction

This research project aims to understand how users experience free live streaming platforms. We are interested in learning about your habits, preferences, and concerns regarding security and privacy on these platforms.

Participate in Our Survey

Please take a few minutes to complete our anonymous survey. Your responses will help us gain valuable insights into this growing phenomenon.

Expected Outcomes

The findings from this survey will be used to inform academic publications and presentations on the topic of free sports live streaming services. We hope this research will contribute to a better understanding of user experiences and promote safer online practices.
