Sina Keshvadi

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Software Engineeting at Thompson Rivers University in Kamploos, BC, Canada.
My office is in Old Main building, # OM 2736.

My research focuses on Computer Networks, Internet Security Measurement, and Educational Technology.
Feel free to contact me at webidsina@gmail.com if you would like to chat or discuss.

I was fortunate be a sessional lecturer and also work with Prof. Carey Williamson as part of the Network Research Group at University of Calgary (2017 - 2021).
Previously, I was a faculty member at the Payame Noor University, Tehran, Iran (2012 - 2017).

I love Network Programming and thinking about fun network security problems.
